Digital, Visual and Product Designer

Pinkie

A location-based app designed to empower women with safer urban mobility through smart, community-driven features.

As the lead designer, I crafted a user interface that prioritizes clarity, trust, and quick access to critical tools. The app provides secure walking routes using verified data, highlights safe public venues, and includes an emergency SOS button for instant contact with authorities. Balancing privacy and security, user registration requires ID verification, while profiles remain private. A key feature is its community-driven reporting system, which allows users to share real-time safety updates, ensuring the app evolves with the needs of its users.

Dr. Safa Mahmoodinejad's veterinary

As a freelance designer, I had the opportunity to create a cohesive brand identity for Dr. Mahmoodinejad's Veterinary Clinic. My work included designing promotional materials such as posters, business cards, and digital advertisements that effectively communicate the clinic's professionalism and care for animals. Using a modern and vibrant color palette, I ensured that the visuals were engaging and approachable while maintaining a sense of trust and credibility. The designs incorporate clean typography, well-structured layouts, and animal-friendly imagery to resonate with pet owners and establish a strong brand presence.

UI/UX Designer - Branding and marketing

Nightlist

As a UI/UX Designer for Nightlist, I contributed to designing a seamless digital experience that helps users navigate Stockholm's nightlife with clarity and confidence. The app was created to address key pain points, such as difficulty finding clubs with the right music, navigating social dynamics, and avoiding wasted time and money. My focus was on designing an intuitive filtering system, allowing users to discover venues based on music preference, age group, and budget. Additionally, I worked on social features that let users connect with like-minded people by making their attendance public and setting their intentions for the night. Throughout the design process, I collaborated closely with UX researchers, project managers, and creative designers to develop a user-friendly and visually engaging interface.

Budi

As a UI/UX Designer for Budi, I played a key role in shaping the brand's digital presence by developing a cohesive and user-friendly interface that aligned with its identity. My work involved designing an intuitive user experience, ensuring seamless navigation, and creating visually appealing layouts that enhance usability. I focused on consistency in branding, utilizing a modern and clean design system that strengthened Budi's recognition.

Through typography, color schemes, and interactive components, I ensured that every design element contributed to a coherent and engaging digital experience. In collaboration with the team, I worked on wireframing, prototyping, and user testing, refining the UI based on insights and feedback.

Lightstep

As a Digital Designer for the LightStep project, I played a key role in crafting a visually engaging and digital experience aimed at supporting young adults on their recovery journey. I collaborated closely with UX designers, researchers, and developers to refine the visual identity and user experience. I was involved in wireframing, prototyping, and iterating based on user testing feedback, ensuring that the design aligned with the needs of the target audience. My contributions also extended to branding and typography choices, emphasizing clarity, warmth, and inclusivity.

DR. Mahmoodinejad's Pharmacy

A Modern Digital & Brand Experience for Dr. Mahmoodnejad's pharmacy, I played a key role in shaping both the user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) of their digital presence while also developing a strong brand identity. My work included designing an e-commerce website that allows customers to browse and purchase pharmaceutical products with ease. Prioritizing accessibility and functionality, I ensured that the website aligns with modern design principles, providing a smooth shopping experience across devices.
